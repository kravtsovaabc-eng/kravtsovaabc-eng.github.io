Square Breathing Practice

Find any square-shaped object around you: a window frame, a picture or a book. Focus your eyes on the upper left corner and slowly inhale, counting to four.

Move your gaze to the upper right corner and hold your breath for four counts. Continue following the square with your eyes, alternating inhale, hold and exhale.

Keep your head still and your muscles relaxed.
